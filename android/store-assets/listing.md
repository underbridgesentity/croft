**App name** (30 max):
Croft - Family Hub

**Short description** (80 max):

**Full description** (4000 max):
Croft brings the moving parts of family life into one calm, shared home - off your group chats.

PLAN TOGETHER
• A shared calendar everyone can see and edit - appointments, school runs, birthdays
• Repeating events, multi-day trips, and reminders that arrive before things happen
• Subscribe in Google Calendar or Apple Calendar with one link

STAY ORGANIZED
• To-dos and reminders with real due dates - assign them to anyone
• Shopping lists the whole family updates live (make as many lists as you need)
• A weekly meal planner with ingredients that jump straight onto the shopping list
• Family goals with target dates - and private personal goals only you can see

HANDLE MONEY, TOGETHER
• Bills with due dates, overdue alerts and autopay tracking
• Monthly budgets with a full spend history and insights
• Savings goals the whole family can add to
• Who-owes-who, settled in taps instead of awkward maths

BUILT FOR FAMILIES
• Everyone gets their own login; invite your family with a link
• Push notifications for the things that matter - new events, bills, nudges, and a calm morning digest
• A friendly daily email summary, if you want it
• Passcode lock for shared devices
• Your household's data is private to your household - no ads, no selling data, delete your account any time

Free to use. Works beautifully on phones, tablets and desktop, always in sync.
